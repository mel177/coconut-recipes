
Buttery Coconut Bars
Buttery Coconut Bars are a sinfully good dessert that's easy to make and always a crowd pleaser!
 Course Dessert
 Prep Time 20 minutes
 Cook Time 40 minutes
 Total Time 1 hour
 Servings 36 bars
 Author Amanda Batcher
Ingredients
CRUST
	•	2 cups all-purpose flour
	•	1 cup brown sugar packed
	•	1 cup butter melted
	•	1/2 tsp salt
FILLING
	•	3 large eggs at room temperature
	•	1 14 oz can sweetened condensed milk
	•	1/2 cup all-purpose flour
	•	1/4 cup brown sugar packed
	•	1/4 cup butter melted
	•	2 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
	•	1/2 tsp salt
	•	4 cups sweetened shredded or flaked coconut, divided

Instructions
	1.	Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line a 13-inch x 9-inch baking pan with parchment paper or aluminum foil and set aside. 
	2.	To a mixing bowl, add melted butter, and brown sugar. Stir together, then add in flour and salt, mixing until dough forms 
	3.	Press dough into the bottom of prepared pan in an even layer. Bake 12-15 minutes, until lightly golden brown. 
	4.	Set aside to cool for 5-10 minutes and reduce oven temperature to 325 degrees F. 
	5.	To a large mixing bowl, add all filling ingredients except coconut and whisk together. Stir in 3 cups of the coconut. 
	6.	Pour filling over cooled crust and sprinkle top with remaining 1 cup of coconut. 
	7.	Bake 25-30 minutes, or until set and coconut is lightly golden brown. 
	8.	Remove from the oven and cool to room temperature. 
	9.	Slice into bars and serve.
